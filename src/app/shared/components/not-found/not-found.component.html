Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my PetFeeder Pro?

To reset your device, locate the reset button at the bottom and hold it for 10 seconds.

 Device shows 'Offline' but Wi-Fi is working?

First, ensure your device is plugged into a reliable power source. If it is, try holding the pairing button on the back for 5 seconds until the LED blinks blue, then re-add the device in your app. If the issue persists, please check your router's 2.4GHz network settings.

 How accurate is the grain level sensor?

The sensor has a precision margin of +/- 5 grams.

 Can I schedule multiple feeding times?

Yes, you can schedule up to 12 feeding times per day via the Schedule tab.
